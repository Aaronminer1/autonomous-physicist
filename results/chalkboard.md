
## Stationary Crane Physics
Force Balance Derivation:
For a mass m suspended by a cable at angle θ from vertical:
∑F_y = 0: T·cos(θ) - m·g = 0
Therefore: T = m·g / cos(θ)

Given: m = 10 kg, g = 9.81 m/s², θ = 30° = π/6 rad
Tension_Value = 10 × 9.81 / cos(π/6) = 98.1 / (√3/2) = 196.2/√3 = 65.4√3 N ≈ 113.28 N

## Crane Mission Alpha
Tension_45: For a 10kg mass at 45-degree angle from vertical
Derivation: T = m*g / cos(θ)
With m = 10 kg, g = 9.81 m/s², θ = 45° = π/4 rad
T = 10 × 9.81 / cos(π/4) = 98.1 / (√2/2) = 98.1 × 2/√2 = 98.1√2 N
Tension_45 = 98.1√2 N ≈ 138.73 N

## Crane Mission Gamma
Tension_60: For a 10kg mass at 60-degree angle from vertical
Derivation: T = m*g / cos(θ)
With m = 10 kg, g = 9.81 m/s², θ = 60° = π/3 rad
T = 10 × 9.81 / cos(60°) = 98.1 / 0.5 = 196.2 N
Tension_60 = 196.2 N
